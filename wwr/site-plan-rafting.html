Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of our rafting business website is to build revenue by attracting new customers and retaining existing ones. The site will showcase our rafting services, unique experiences, and allow for easy online booking.

Audience

Our rafting business targets adventure enthusiasts, nature lovers, and families seeking unique outdoor experiences. The target customers, aged 18-50, generally have a moderate to high disposable income, enjoy physical activities, and appreciate nature. They may be seasoned rafters or beginners seeking adventure. Our customers want detailed information about rafting trips, including difficulty levels, duration, costs, and necessary gear. They expect to find comprehensive trip descriptions, customer testimonials, safety information, and easy online booking options. Key questions such as "What are the best trips for beginners?", "What safety measures are in place?", and "Are there family-friendly options?" will be clearly addressed. Most of our audience will access the site via mobile devices and laptops, with mobile responsiveness and seamless user experience across all devices being essential to meet their needs and expectations.
